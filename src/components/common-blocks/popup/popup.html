PACIFIC STUDY

Key Inclusion Criteria2

  • 18 years of age or older
  • WHO performance status ≤1
  • Life expectancy ≥12 weeks
  • Recent (≤3 months) tumor biopsy and archived tumor tissue block

Key Exclusion Criteria2

  • Any unresolved prior toxicity of CTCAE >Grade 2
  • Any prior ≥Grade 3 imAE, or any unresolved imAE >Grade 1
  • Patients with ≥Grade 2 pneumonitis from prior chemoradiation therapy
  • Active or prior autoimmune disease requiring systemic treatment or inflammatory bowel disease
  • History of immunodeficiency, allogeneic organ transplant, or tuberculosis
  • Current or prior use of immunosuppressive medication within 28 days before the first dose
  • Uncontrolled intercurrent illness
  • Prior exposure to any anti-PD-1 or anti-PD-L1 antibody

anti-PD-1: anti programmed death-1; anti-PD-L1: anti-programmed death ligand-1; CTCAE: Common Terminology Criteria for Adverse Events; imAE: immune-mediated AEs.
